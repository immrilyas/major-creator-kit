About Major Creator Kit

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to democratize Instagram success. In the competitive world of social media, standing out is everything. We believe that every creator, influencer, and small business owner deserves access to high-quality tools that can help them grow, without the high price tag.

Major Creator Kit was built to be a one-stop-shop for all your Instagram needs. From generating the perfect bio and hashtags with AI, to planning your grid and checking for banned hashtags, we provide reliable, fast, and free tools to make your social media management easier and more effective.
